Dan Cascaval

Third year undergraduate at Carnegie Mellon studying Architecture and Computer Science. I am looking for an opportunity to gain industry experience integrating design and technology as part of a project or team. I have experience and am particularly interested in working with graphics, geometry, and human-computer interaction.

In my existing research, I take the design components of an architecture education and mesh them with computational techniques in architectural research - combining physical simulations and designer input to generate a method for robots to fabricate complex concrete surfaces and create programmable environments. Results of this work have been submitted to a variety of journals and conferences for publication.

Always open to opportunities involving engineering software with a focus on integrating computation, design, and sustainability.
