I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Washington advised by Ras Bodik and Adriana Schulz.
I am working on problems in computational geometry and shape manipulation using program synthesis and related tools. Right now, that includes representing 3D CAD models as programs and developing synthesis-based tools for bidirectional editing, constraint inference, and feature transfer. I am always looking for new applications for this work, particularly in the domain of improving architecture, design, and the built environment.
